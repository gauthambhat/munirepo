POD Information

Print on Demand Services

eBooks2go, Inc has partnered with several printers to offer quality printed books, in single or four colors, utilizing the cost effective POD (Print On Demand) model. POD allows you to print as few as 1 copy or up to 1000 copies in as little as two weeks. Not only that, with POD you can inexpensively produce Advanced Reader Copies (ARCs) of your book, or part of your book, for use as a great marketing tool. The POD model takes away the cost of printing and warehousing large quantities of books before they are needed for a sales transaction.

Our print partners are strategically located throughout the world significantly reducing shipping costs for authors and publishers as printing will take place as close to the book purchaser as possible.

1. Book Trim size – POD offers many sizes but unlike offset printing the trim sizes are limited. (All sizes are in inches)

  • 5 X 8
  • 5½ X 8½
  • 6 X 9
  • 6¼ X9¼
  • 7 X 10

2. Color – Will your book print in color or black and white?
3. Binding – Will your book be hard bound or soft bound?
4. Dust Jackets - Do you want your book to have a dust jacket? (Hard bound editions only)
5. Paper Color - Natural or White color paper is available.
6. Quantity
7. Delivery Destination (this will assist us in determining which of our print partners is closest to your customers to reduce your shipping costs)
