eBook Royalties

For Amazon:
For eBooks priced between $2.99 and $9.99
  • 70% for sales in Andorra, Austria, Belgium, Canada, France, Great Britain, Guernsey, Germany, Italy, Isle of Man, Jersey, Liechtenstein, Luxembourg, Monaco, San Marino, Switzerland, Spain and the United States (minus a delivery fee determined by Amazon based on the size of your book)
  • 35% of sales in all other countries.
For eBooks priced below $2.99 and above $9.99
  • If you price your book higher or lower than that suggested range, you keep 35% of all sales in all countries.
For B&N:
Author / Publisher will set a List Price for each eBook between $0.99 and $199.99. Author / Publisher will be paid a royalty off the List Price according to the following terms:
  • For eBooks with a List Price at or between $2.99 and $9.99 65% of the List Price
  • For eBooks with a List Price at or below $2.98 or at or greater than $10.00 (but not more than $199.99 and not less than $0.99) 40% of the List Price

For Kobo:
  • Kobo is very similar to Apple. Kobo keeps 30 percent and pays 70 percent irrespective of the sales price or the territory.
For Sony:
  • Sony is very similar to Apple and Kobo, keeps 30 percent of the Sale price and pays 70 percent as royalty to Author/Publisher for sales anywhere in the world.
For eBooks2go:
  • eBooks2go keeps 30 percent and pays 70 percent of the sale price as royalty to Author/Publisher.
