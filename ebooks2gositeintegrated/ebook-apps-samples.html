Malay Speak Easy

Malay Speak Easy App is developed with the goal to introduce briefly Malay Language to Tourists coming from all over the world to Malaysia located in South East part of Asia. This App contains most commonly used words and sentences in daily life . The audio feature translates words and sentences in English and Malay. Learning little bit about these words and sentences will make visitors quickly connect with local population. We feel, this app will make the stay of the Tourists little more pleasant.
