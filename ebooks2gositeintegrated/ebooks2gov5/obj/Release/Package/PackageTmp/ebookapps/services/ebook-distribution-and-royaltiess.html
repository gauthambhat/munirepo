eBook Distribution & Royalties

eBooks2go offers eBook distribution services to Author & Publishers. Our current retail partners include Amazon, Barnes & Nobel, Apple’s iBook store, Google, Sony, Kobo, Over Drive and and our own store eBooks2go. When you sign up with eBooks2go for conversion services, you can choose to distribute your book through us or directly yourself.

When you chose to distribute your eBook through us, we will upload your approved eBook file to all the retail partner stores and make it available throughout the world. Unlike many conversion houses who charge upfront money for distribution, eBooks2go operates on a small percentage (10 Percent of the list price of eBook) of agency commission for acting as your distribution agent.

How Author / Publisher Royalties are calculated for eBook sales?

An example is provided to show how much an Author / Publisher gets paid on eBook Sales

Amazon Kindle Store  
  • Amazon Provides options two options for selling your Books
  • 70 percent royalty to Authors - Under this option, the list price must be between $2.99 and $9.99 and you cannot run free price promotions. Amazon reserves the right to discount the eBook, pay royalties based on the discounted price and charge transaction fee downloads of your eBook. Amazon also charges higher fee (65 percent) if the book is sold in non Amazon territories
  • 35 percent royalty to Authors – Under this option, Amazon takes 65 percent of the royalty and pays the author/publisher 35 percent of the list price irrespective of the price of your eBook and the territory in which it gets sold. It absorbs the file download costs, promotional discounts etc.
    Example Under 70 percent option USD ($) Under 35 percent option USD ($)
     eBook retail download price $ 9 .99 $ 9 .99
     Amazon Royalties $ 3 .00 $ 6 .50
     eBooks2go distribution fee $ 0 .99 $ 0 .99
     Net royalties to Author $ 5 .99 $ 2 .50
    Barnes & Noble  
  • Barnes & Nobel provides two options
  • Option 1 : For eBooks with a List Price at or between $2.99 and $9.99 65% of the List Price
  • Option 2 : For eBooks with a List Price at or below $2.98 or at or greater than $10.00 (but not more than $199.99 and not less than $0.99) 40% of the List Price
    Example Option 1 Option 2
