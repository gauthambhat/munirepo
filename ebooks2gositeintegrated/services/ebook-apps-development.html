eBook Apps

What are eBook Apps?

The Publishing & Media industry has been revolutionized by the introduction of Smart phones and Tablets with Mobile Apps and their use is growing exponentially worldwide. The Apps that run on these devices are redefining the traditional ways of reading and learning more than ever before. Today we have access to Music, News, Interactive Games, Learning & Education and so much more through this new medium of Mobile Apps.

This is a great opportunity for Authors, Content providers and Educators to offer rich content using the Audio, Video, Interactive and Social Media features possible in eBook Apps to reach out to millions of readers worldwide and build lasting relationships with their global audiences.

Our eBook Reader App

We, at eBooks2go, have developed an "eBook App Reader" that supports multiple input file formats such as PDF, Word, Image and XML to publish your books as eBook Apps on iPhone, iPod, iPad and Android platforms.

While almost any book can be published as an eBook App, we highly recommend choosing eBook Apps for publishing Children's Books, Work Books, Cook Books, Self - Learning Books, Professional Books, Travel Guides, Music Guides, Photo Albums, Testing & Assessment Books, Flash Cards, Product Manuals, and Company Annual Reports etc.

Whether it is simple Book-to-eBook App conversion or designing a highly interactive content rich eBook App, we can help you. Our pre-built templates can help customize your books quickly while our team of highly skilled designers, programmers, media editors and publishing prof8essionals build your books rapidly with an eye for detail one page at a time. Once the Books are built as eBook Apps and are approved by you, we’ll publish them on Apple’s App Store and Google's Market Place making them available to millions of reader worldwide. You can monitor and track your eBook App Sales through your account on a daily, weekly, monthly or quarterly basis.

eBook Reader Application features:

  • Gaming functionality
  • Full color/Audio/Video
  • Social Media: By adding social media links you can interact with your readers around the world to talk about your books and promote upcoming signings, speaking engagements and more.
