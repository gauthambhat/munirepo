Enhanced eBooks for iPad

Apple has released special software called iBooks Author to create and publish amazing Multi-Touch Books for iPad. Using this software, we can build beautiful Multi-Touch textbooks with features such as Photo galleries, Video, interactive diagrams, 3D objects, mathematical expressions, quizzes and more bringing content to life in ways the printed page never could.

Using this software eBooks2go has created Text Books, Travel guides, Picture Books, Cook Books, Scientific, Technical and Mathematical Books and published them as enhanced eBooks with iBook Store.

If you have just one Book or Series of Books, our team will work with you to create an immersive, interactive and engaging multi-touch book that will capture readers imaginations and take learning to a whole new level.


We understand how important the quality of conversion is for Authors and publishers. We pride ourselves on our service and conversion quality. Our conversions go through various stages of proofing, before sending the files back to you.

Once the enhanced eBook is approved from your end, we will help you with uploading of the eBook into iBookstore or we can manage the distribution & Pay royalties for you.
