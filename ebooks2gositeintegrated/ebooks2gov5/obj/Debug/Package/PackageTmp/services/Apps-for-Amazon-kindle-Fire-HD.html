Apps for Amazon Kindle Fire HD

What is the Kindle Fire

Amazon’s Kindle Fire is a tablet computer version of their Kindle eBook reader. It runs a modified version of Google’s Android operating system so allowing users to run and source apps originally intended for Android devices. Amazon currently offers two variations of tablets with stunning 7" and 8.9" high-definition displays, and the all-new Kindle Fire!

Kindle Fire is the #1 best-selling product among Android based tablets and developing your content for Kindle Fire allows you to grow your business by engaging new customers and monetizing your app.

Amazon Kindle Fire HD device gives instant access to millions of top most popular free and best-selling paid apps and games in the App Store.

Our skilled developers will understand the differences between Android App development and Kindle Fire App development platform to customize apps according to ideas.

Developing Apps for Kindle Fire HD

The Kindle Fire runs on a modified version of Android Gingerbread Operating system which is slightly different from Android system. If you have already developed apps for Android, with slight modifications, we can make them work on Kindle Fire devices. Our Android developers understand specific differences between Kindle Fire development platform and Android development platform customize your apps accordingly.
