Enhanced eBook Services

Moving the reader from “reading” to “experiencing”

With the improvements in recent eBook technology, readers have become more comfortable with " non-printed" books and there has been an increased demand for "Enhanced eBooks". The most dominant "enhancements" are the introduction of audio and video files into the "linear" story line of the book, something that cannot be done in a printed book.

If designed well, enhanced eBooks can be engaging and captivating. With the introduction of EPUB3 standards, eBooks2go now proudly offers authors, publishers, content providers of all sizes the ability to take their content to the next level

Here are different types of enhanced eBook solutions available from us today -

Enhanced eBook-Fixed Layout Format

If you have a Book where content and design are so intertwined that they can’t be separated or any change in appearance risks changing the meaning or losing all meaning, then your best choice is to go with fixed layout format using ePUB3 standards. This format gives you greater control over content presentation. Some of the features include Pop up text/images, Footnote, internal and external links etc

Not all reading devices in the market today support Fixed Layout formats. The Most popular devices that support this format are iPad, Kindle Fire, latest Nook devices. Some of the Reader Apps such as Kobo for Android and iPad do support Fixed layout formats.

This format is best for Children’s illustrated Books, Picture Books, Coffee table books, Cook Books, Text Books and nonfiction books.

Enhanced eBook – With Video / Audio

Today’s ePUB Technology supports adding videos / audios to your eBooks. If you are planning an enhanced eBook with Video / Audio or currently have an eBook and would like to enhance it by adding rich multimedia functions, internal/external links, foot notes etc, eBooks2go can help you realize goal.

Not all reading devices in the market today support ePUB files with Videos / Audios. The Most popular devices that support this format are iPad, Kindle Fire HD and recent Nook devices.

This format is best for Non-Fiction books, Educational Books, Travel Guides, Maintenance Manuals, Cook Books etc

Enhanced eBook- Illustrated with Animation and Read Aloud

Fixed Layout Read Aloud books feature audio that can be selected to high light text and read the eBook aloud. You can embed Video, 2D or 3D Animation if needed. You can also add Popup Text, Images as slide shows, links and foot notes etc. These features can take the reader from “reading” to “experiencing” the book.

Enhanced eBooks with these features today are supported mainly by iPad.

This format is best for Educational & Children’s Books.

Our team of Designers, Software experts, Artists and Content developers will work with you to transform your ideas and content into great looking enhanced eBooks.
