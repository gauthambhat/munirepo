eBook Conversion Services

What is an "eBook"?

Ebook is a user-friendly concept that facilitates the user to personalize and make the most out of their reading experience.

It is a simple electronic rendition of your printed book that is converted into a standard format, typically epub, allowing it to be "read" on any number of electronic reading devices, smart phones or computers.

Why convert your printed book to an eBook?

Converting your book is a very cost-efficient way of making your book available on a multitude of eReading devices, from a variety of eRetailers, to tens of millions of readers worldwide. There is progressively more massive demand for English-language eBooks all over the world.
