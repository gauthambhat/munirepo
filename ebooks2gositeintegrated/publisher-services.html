Publisher Services

eBooks2go, Inc. provides content conversion, publishing and online distribution services to publishers worldwide. publishers are facing enormous challenges as result of rapid technological changes in the on-line publishing world. Keeping pace with these changes requires not only continuous investments in time and resources but also a very clear understanding of which digital strategy to be pursued to maximize sales and profits.

With eBooks2go as your content conversion and distribution partner, you have instant access to a team of highly experienced digital publishing professionals to guide you through the various digital publishing standards and help you chose the right strategy based on specific content types and business goals.

For example we can take your backlist of titles and quickly convert them to ePub and/or .mobi (Kindle) formats for rapid entry into the eBook markets. Or we can work with your editorial and marketing teams to evaluate specific titles that could benefit from the addition of video, audio or additional enhancements. Perhaps you have children’s or academic titles that could become multi-level interactive products? Again by talking to our specialists we will be able to plan an XML workflow to move you in the right direction.

Our team will work with you closely to define and build a sustainable digital strategy so you won’t have to make any excessive investments in digital infrastructure.
